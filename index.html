About Me .

Delightful and enthralling, I am a 14 year old hoarder of endless curiosity, a perfectionist's conundrum, and a lover of peculiarities and anomalies. My inscrutable visage is true to my character- tight lipped, with eyes that might be kindly and slightly sad, content yet ever impatient for the next adventure. I was raised in an environment where i was encouraged to be different, to get lost in my own mind and take that voyage, even if it was down the path with heartbreak. To me life is worth living it without regrets.
